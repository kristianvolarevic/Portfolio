About Me

Hi! I am a university student in my final semester of a Bachelor of Information Technology. I am majoring in Games and Immersive Media, learning the fundamentals of the industry. I currently live and study in Melbourne, Australia.

My strengths lie in programming and problem-solving, but I am also well versed in 3D modelling, with some limited experience in sound design. Having grown up playing many video games, sharing this experience with others is a passion of mine, and it's what drew me to the field of game development.

In my free time, I love coming up with ideas for games I might create often ambitiously scoping them far too large as my imagination runs wild. This creativity stems from my love of comic books, TV shows, and movies. I've spent countless hours immersing myself in various stories, which has deepened my appreciation for the people who work within these industries.
